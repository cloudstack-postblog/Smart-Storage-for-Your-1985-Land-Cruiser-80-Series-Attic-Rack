Efficiently Utilize Overhead Space in Your Land Cruiser

We designed this rack to solve a specific problem we’ve faced ourselves: wasted headroom in the rear of the Land Cruiser. Instead of letting that space go unused, we created a lightweight, low-profile attic rack that mounts just under the headliner. It offers a sturdy yet sleek platform for soft goods like jackets, sleeping bags, backpacks, recovery gear, or camp supplies all without interfering with your rear visibility or headroom.

This is especially useful when the rest of your vehicle is loaded up or when you want quick access to high-use items that don’t belong buried in your trunk.

Unlike bulky storage bins or loose cargo that shifts around, our attic rack keeps everything securely in place using a simple net system (included). It’s the kind of solution that you only have to install once, but you’ll rely on every day.
